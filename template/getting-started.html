How to change the phone mockup

1. Request Figma Files

Original Figma files are reserved for customers only. Please email us your proof of purchase and allow our team to prepare and send these to you. Inside of the figma files you can easily edit the screen UI to suit your name and recent design projects and apply it to the phone mockup.

Change or Remove Page Loading

1. Find the Page Load Interaction, here you can remove or change it.
2. Find the page load symbol
3. Change its display to flex so its visible
4. Edit it as you require. Then change it back to hidden
